How to take photos (so we can quote faster)

Quick guide for residential ductless mini‑split service.

← Back 5 photos list →
1) Indoor model label (most important)
  • Take a close, sharp photo of the sticker/label
  • Make sure the model number is readable
2) Outdoor unit surroundings
  • Step back so we can see clearance and piping direction
  • Include anything blocking airflow (leaves, snow, fence, etc.)
3) Leak / water marks
  • Photo where water drips from
  • Photo of any stains on wall/ceiling
4) Video (10 seconds)
  • Capture the problem: sound, leak, weak airflow
  • Keep the phone steady
