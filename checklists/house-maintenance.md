# Mini‑Split Maintenance Checklist (Homeowner‑Safe) — House Version

> **Scope**: Residential ductless mini‑split systems.
>
> **Rules** (important):
> - Do **not** open electrical panels.
> - Do **not** pressure‑wash the outdoor coil/fins.
> - If you’re unsure, **stop** and call a professional.

---

## 中文版（屋主可执行｜独栋/house 版本）

### 每月（或使用频繁时每 2–4 周）
1) **清洗滤网（室内机）**
- 取下滤网 → 清水冲洗 → 阴干 → 装回
- 不要湿滤网直接装回去

2) **确保回风/出风不被遮挡**
- 家具、窗帘、杂物不要挡住进/出风口

3) **观察是否有异常**
- 异味（霉味/酸味）
- 滴水/漏水
- 声音异常（嗡嗡/咔哒/尖叫）
- 制冷/制热明显变差

### 换季前（夏前/冬前）
4) **室外机周围清理（独栋常见）**
- 室外机四周至少留出 **60cm（约 2 英尺）**空间
- 清除落叶、杂草、塑料袋、积雪
- **不要用高压水枪**冲洗翅片（易损坏/进水）

5) **检查排水/潮湿痕迹**
- 室内机、管线、墙面是否有水迹/霉点
- 一旦发现异常：**先停机并拍照/录像**，再联系专业人员

### 每年（建议专业保养 1 次）
6) **专业深度清洗 + 性能检查**
- 深度清洗蒸发器/风轮/排水盘/排水管
- 检查温差、风量、系统运行状态

## English (homeowner‑safe) — House version

### Monthly (or every 2–4 weeks in heavy use)
1) **Wash indoor filters**
- Rinse → air‑dry → reinstall
- Don’t reinstall while wet

2) **Keep air inlet/outlet clear**
- No curtains/furniture blocking airflow

3) **Watch for early warning signs**
- Moldy odor
- Water leaks/drips
- Unusual noises
- Weak cooling/heating

### Seasonal (before summer/winter)
4) **Outdoor unit clearance (common for houses)**
- Keep ~ **2 ft (60 cm)** around the unit
- Remove leaves/snow/debris
- **No pressure washer** on fins/coils

5) **Check for drainage issues**
- Any wet marks near indoor units / lineset
- If abnormal: **turn off** and capture photos/video

### Yearly (recommended)
6) **Professional deep cleaning + inspection**
- Coil/blower wheel/drain pan & line
- Performance check

---

## Call a pro ASAP if… / 需要立刻找专业人员的情况
- Breaker trips, burning smell, smoke
- Significant leaks (water damage risk)
- Persistent strong odor
- Severe icing / frequent shutdown
