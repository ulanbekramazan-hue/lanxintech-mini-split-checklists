Mini‑Split Maintenance Checklist — House version

Residential ductless mini‑splits • homeowner‑safe

Rules:
  • Do not open electrical panels.
  • No pressure washer on the outdoor coil/fins.
  • If you smell burning or see breaker trips: shut off and call a pro.

中文（每月 / 换季 / 每年）

每月（或使用频繁时每 2–4 周）

  • 清洗滤网：清水冲洗 → 阴干 → 装回（不要湿装）
  • 保持回风/出风通畅：别被家具窗帘遮挡
  • 观察异常：异味、滴水、噪音、制冷/制热变差

换季前（夏前/冬前）

  • 室外机四周留出约 60cm（2 英尺）空间，清理落叶/积雪/杂物
  • 检查墙面/管线附近是否潮湿或发霉；异常先停机拍照/录像

每年（建议专业保养 1 次）

  • 专业深度清洗：蒸发器/风轮/排水盘/排水管
  • 性能检查：温差、风量、运行状态

English

Monthly (or every 2–4 weeks in heavy use)

  • Wash filters: rinse → air‑dry → reinstall (don’t reinstall wet)
  • Keep inlet/outlet clear (no curtains/furniture blocking airflow)
  • Watch for odor, leaks, unusual noises, weak cooling/heating

Seasonal (before summer/winter)

  • Outdoor clearance: keep ~2 ft (60 cm) around unit; remove leaves/snow/debris
  • If you see moisture/water marks: turn off and capture photos/video

Yearly (recommended)

  • Professional deep clean (coil/blower/drain) + performance check
